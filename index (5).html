IEEE Published · 2025
Enhanced Myocardial Infarction Prediction through ECG using Multimodal Transfer Learning
Conference
2025 AI-Driven Smart Healthcare for Society 5.0
Date
14–15 February 2025
Location
Kolkata, India
DOI
10.1109/IEEECONF64992.2025.10962950
Publisher
IEEE Xplore
Omana J  ·  Sanjay Akash R V  ·  Harish Anand  ·  Sujay Sai R
Proposes a novel deep learning approach to predict complications of myocardial infarction by analyzing ECG images. A hybrid model integrating Xception and ResNet50 extracts diverse features from ECG scans, processed through concatenation, batch normalization, dropout, and dense layers. L2 regularization applied to prevent overfitting; Adam and SGD optimizers used. Evaluated with accuracy, F1 score, confusion matrix, and ROC curves. A Streamlit web application enables real-time prediction and visualization for clinicians.
